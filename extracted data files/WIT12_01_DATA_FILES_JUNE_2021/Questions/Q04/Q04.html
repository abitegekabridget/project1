Why Bees Matter

Bees are essential to our ecosystem. They pollinate plants, ensuring food production and biodiversity. Without them, many species would struggle to survive.

Challenges Facing Bees

Climate change, habitat loss, and pesticides have led to a decline in bee populations.

Efforts are being made worldwide to protect and conserve bee populations. Planting wildflowers, reducing pesticide use, and supporting local beekeepers can all help.
